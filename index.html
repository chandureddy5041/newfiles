About Us

Indian Career Zone(ICZ) is providing the most up-to-date and relevant guidance materials to those needing or providing career guidance.

Career Guidance facilitates people throughout their lives to manage their own educational, training, occupational, personal, social and life choices so that they reach their full potential and contribute to the development of a better society.

ICZ is an educational training sector with a focus on test prep segment and also helps freshers & experienced get job.

ICZ provides information, advice and guidance to help you make decisions on learning, training and work opportunities. The service offers confidential and impartial advice. This is supported by qualified careers advisers.

How We Help You?

We know that everyone has the ability and potential to develop and grow in their lives and careers. We also understand that sometimes things get in the way and can hold you back.
Our Aim:
Career Planning

Help you with career decisions and planning.

Develop Skills

Support you in reviewing your skills and abilities and develop new goals.

Plan of Action

Motivate you to implement your plan of action.

Career Tools

Enable you to make the best use of high quality career related tools.

Career Help

Have you been made redundant and are unsure of your next move? Are you returning to work from time out? We can help.




Perhaps you have just finished college or university and are considering your next move or you might be unhappy in your current job and ready for a change, but not sure what. You might have been out of work for a while and looking for job hunting tips.

Whatever may be the situation or whatever may be the barriers, we are here to help you overcome them and reach your potential. We also realise that jobs these days need more skills and qualifications. Hence we are here to help you build on your strengths, improve your weaker areas, and help you find a job or career that suits your skills and personality.

Our Advisers

Our expert advisers are knowledgeable, enthusiastic and provide independent help. They aim to empower you to make your own career and learning choices.

Our advisers are trained to inspire and motivate you. They provide you with the information, advice and guidance you need to help you take control of your learning and working life.
Our advisers put you and your needs at the heart of the process. They help you make the best possible decisions. We offer advice on learning and work issues, help you explore which careers would suit you best, provide tips on searching for a job and help you to improve your interview skills.

Your Career Adviser Can Help You With:

1) Develop your Curriculum Vitae (CV).

2) Search and apply for jobs.

3) Understand the job market.

4) Search for courses and training schemes.

5) Find funding to support any learning.




6) Identify your key strengths and skills.

7) Explore your career options.

8) Choose training routes that fit your lifestyle.

9) Develop an action plan - this can help you achieve your goals.

10) Refer you to other useful organisations.
